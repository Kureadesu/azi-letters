Cornelia Street (Part 2)

February 6, 2026 Bagong Silangan / San Mateo

I love sunsets. I love how the sky turn into a canvas of colors, and how the sun slowly disappears below the horizon. I love how the world seems to slow down during that time, and how everything feels more peaceful. The hill you've shown me is a great spot to watch the sunset. It felt peaceful when you brought me there, away from the noise and chaos of the city. It was one of those moments where words weren't necessary, and we just enjoyed the beauty of the sky together. The challenge this time is if I can handle myself when I pass by your house. I don't know what would I feel when I see you by the window, and I was hoping you would go out and do some errands or something. Anyway, I just want to watch the sunset so I travelled quick after my shift.

I arrived in Sandigan just in time, and I walked from the school to the hill. I stopped by the store in front of your home to buy water, while subtly looking at your window. I guess you were laying on the couch, bed-rotting. I continued walking until I reached the hill, and sat down at our spot. I loved the breeze and the quiet. The sunset was beautiful as always. It's peaceful to be there, and it would be even better if you were there with me. While I was there, a group of teenagers were jamming together. They were playing the guitar and singing 'Tensionado', what a vibe to my emotions. I didn't stay for long. I went downhill as the sky went dark and I bought myself some street food before I travel home.

I got to see you again when I stopped by your house. I miss you Azi, but all I can do is to watch you from afar. I felt at peace when I saw you, not any pain not bitterness. I am happy you are doing well and being yourself. Few moments later you stood up and I immediately stepped aside where you can't see me. I don't want you to see me as I don't want to feel like a burden, forcing you to come out and talk to me. I don't need that, because I came for the sunset. But because I saw you, it made my day better. Seeing you at peace made me feel peaceful too.

Eli.

With all my heart, forever yours
